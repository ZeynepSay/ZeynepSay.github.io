PharmSci: Description back to ToC

PharmSci provides open source ontology for Medical Science researchers. PharmSci defined the specific Diseases related to Experiment method with the Drugs or Genes used in that clinical study. Clinical Trials used in Clinical Study, and Specimen taken from trials and treatments they exposed to. Experiment methods like Assays, Blot Analysis, Experiment Setting used in the study. Treatment type, but also treatment details, failure or success reason. Study Findings, Evidence, Suggestion of the clinical study.
